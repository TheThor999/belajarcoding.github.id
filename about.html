ABOUT

Gereja Bethel Indonesia Representative Of Christ Kingdom


Pdt. Timotius Arifin Tedjasukmana, Senior Pastor of the GBI ROCK Ministry, was born in Surabaya, June 28, 1951. Husband of his most beloved wife, Fifi Sarah Yasaputra, has spawned and raised many spiritual sons as heirs of the Kingdom of the Lord Jesus Christ. Known as an anointed movement preacher, with the hearts of an evangelist and apostle, Mr. Arifin brings a fresh anointing to praise and worship movement in big cities like Surabaya and Denpasar. Starting church ministry in Bali 1989 with only 5 families in Denpasar, currently he pastor around 45,000 congregations spread over nearly 300 branches of R.O.C.K. Ministries in Indonesia, Asia, Middle East, America and Australia. Currently Mr. Arifin is engaged in the Father-Son or Fatherhood-Sonship ministry which focuses on the urgent need for both young and senior leaders of spiritual fathers. His longing is to awaken spiritual fathers who will be spiritual mentors and fathers of the leaders.

Jln. Raya Glowong (Depan Beras SB), Banyuwangi, Gambiran, Banyuwangi Regency, East Java
